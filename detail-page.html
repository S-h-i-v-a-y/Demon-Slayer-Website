Cruelty

Tanjiro Kamado is a kind-hearted boy who lives in the mountains with his family. He makes a living selling charcoal. One day, when he comes back from selling charcoal, he finds his house engulfed in flames. His family is slaughtered by demons, and his sister Nezuko is the only one left alive.

However, Nezuko has been turned into a demon herself. Desperate to save his sister, Tanjiro learns that she can still be saved. This tragic event marks the beginning of Tanjiro's journey as a Demon Slayer, where he vows to become strong enough to turn Nezuko back to human.

This episode introduces the harsh realities of the world and the cruelty of demons, setting the stage for an epic adventure filled with determination, friendship, and sacrifice.
