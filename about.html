About Demon Slayer

Our Story

Demon Slayer tells the story of Tanjiro Kamado, a young boy whose family was slaughtered by demons. His sister Nezuko is the only survivor, but she's been turned into a demon herself. Refusing to give up, Tanjiro embarks on an epic journey to become a Demon Slayer and find a way to cure his sister.

The anime follows Tanjiro's training, battles, and growth as he meets powerful allies and faces formidable enemies. It explores themes of perseverance, family bonds, friendship, and the struggle between humanity and darkness. With stunning animation and compelling storytelling, Demon Slayer has captivated millions of fans worldwide.
